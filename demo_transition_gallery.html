Issues

  • Regarding percentage widths and heights, Chrome has more visible rounding issues than Firefox, though both will tend to look better when the scaled images happen to have both an even height and width.
  • Chrome will not get the image aspect ratio on document ready. We have to use window load to initialize the slider or hardcode the aspect ratio for the particular set of images. Could probably make setting the aspect ratio an option.
